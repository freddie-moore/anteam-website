Last Mile Optimisation

NHS Hospitals UK

During an 8 week trial, Anteam worked closely with NHS Hospitals and a major UK pharmacy chain. Anteam’s patent pending route optimisation algorithm was deployed to optimise last mile deliveries in the network. The algorithm efficiently processed over 100,000 weekly deliveries split over 1000 vehicles, and was able to deliver huge efficiency gains for the stakeholders involved.

AI Quoting Calculator

Baxter Freight

Anteam developed an AI-powered quoting calculator for the freight-forwarding company Baxter Freight. The tool analyses historical order data and analyses patterns to generate accurate, data-driven quotes, with supplier recommendations in real time. By replacing the manual calculation and research process, Baxter Freight is able to respond to customer enquiries faster, more consistently and with a reduced risk for human error.

Route Optimisation

Welch Group

Anteam applied their advanced route-matching algorithm as an intelligent layer on top of Welch’s existing scheduling systems. The algorithm analyses planned routes and delivery schedules to identify inefficient empty legs.

By detecting these gaps, the system automatically matches them with suitable orders from other suppliers, effectively filling unused capacity and maximising vehicle utilisation. This not only reduces wasted mileage and fuel consumption but also unlocks new revenue opportunities from journeys that would previously generate no return.
